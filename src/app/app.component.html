how it started for me

Like many others, my journey to becoming an engineer has a cliche Lego origin story. I too spent countless hours flipping through instruction manuals to build what was pictured on a box. The feeling of satisfaction after a build is hard to match, but the real magic happened after I got bored playing with the original design.
According to Wikipedia, “the term engineering is derived from the Latin ingenium, meaning “cleverness” and ingeniare, meaning “to contrive, devise”. After I exhausted the novelty in a Lego set, I disassembled it and used the pieces to translate my own imagination into something tactile, ultimately incubating an engineering spirit that would persist for the rest of my life.
