who's kevin?

kevin

/ KEV-in /
proper noun

Kevin Costner was the main character
of the 1990 film Dances with Wolves.
In 2001, my parents named their first born after him
hoping that he would develop the same
down-to-earth, sensitive, individualist
Costner was so well known for.

gu (顾)

/ goo /
surname

In Chinese, 顾 means "to care for".
As a kid I was self-conscious about
my last name as it spawned
nicknames like "googoo".
but when I realized they came out of affection
and not derogatory, I stopped caring.
My last name is quite a literal reminder to
remember what is important and to judge
both sides of the coin
- things I should care for
things I don't need to care for.

As a young adult in a time where everything changes so fast, I am still grappling with how to answer this question. There are a few things I know and/or found out about myself, and I highlighted some on this page.
