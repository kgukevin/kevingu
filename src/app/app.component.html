who's kevin?

My parents named me Kevin after becoming a fan of actor Kevin Costner from watching the film adaptation of Dances with the Wolves.

As a native to the suburbs of Northern Virginia, I grew up in close proximity to humbling natural landscapes and fantastic cityscapes.

From being allowed to explore the outdoors on my own at a young age, I developed a deep appreciation of nature.

Being Chinese-American I had the opportunity to experience two cultures and expand my worldview.

I am an extremely curious person.
